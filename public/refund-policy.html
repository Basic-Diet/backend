basicdite Refund, Return & Cancellation Policy

Effective: May 20, 2026

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Version: 1.0

Target audience: Ages 13+

basicdite ("we", "us", or "our") operates the basicdite mobile application and related meal subscription, pickup, and delivery services. This Refund, Return & Cancellation Policy explains how cancellations, refunds, returns, replacements, skips, freezes, and subscription adjustments are handled.

1. Service Nature

basicdite provides fresh meals, meal subscriptions, meal selections, premium meal upgrades, add-ons, branch pickup, and delivery services.

Because meals are prepared based on customer selections, subscription schedules, daily kitchen operations, and food safety requirements, most confirmed orders and subscriptions are not eligible for cash refunds or physical returns.

2. No Cash Refund Policy

Once a meal order, subscription, premium meal upgrade, add-on, pickup request, or delivery request is confirmed and/or paid, payments are generally non-refundable.

This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Active meal subscriptions
  • Used or partially used subscriptions
  • Prepared meals
  • Meals reserved for branch pickup
  • Meals sent out for delivery
  • Premium meal upgrades
  • Add-ons
  • Missed pickup or delivery attempts caused by customer unavailability or incorrect customer information

Instead of cash refunds, basicdite may offer operational alternatives where applicable, such as:

  • Freezing subscription days
  • Skipping selected days
  • Extending the subscription period
  • Adding grace days
  • Providing replacement meals
  • Providing internal account credit
  • Rescheduling eligible meals

These alternatives are subject to basicdite approval, operational availability, and the status of the meal or subscription.

3. Food Return Policy

Due to food safety and hygiene reasons, meals cannot be physically returned after they have been prepared, delivered, collected, or made available for pickup.

basicdite does not accept returned meals for resale, exchange, or refund.

4. Subscription Cancellation

Meal subscriptions cannot be cancelled for a cash refund after payment confirmation.

If a customer cannot continue using a subscription, basicdite may, at its discretion, provide one or more of the following alternatives:

  • Freeze the subscription
  • Skip upcoming days
  • Extend the subscription validity
  • Add grace days
  • Reschedule remaining eligible meals

Used subscription days, fulfilled meals, no-show pickup requests, and delivery attempts may be counted as consumed and will not be converted into a cash refund.

5. Freeze, Skip & Grace Days

Customers may request to freeze or skip eligible subscription days before meal preparation begins.

Approval depends on:

  • The subscription type
  • The requested date
  • Kitchen preparation status
  • Pickup or delivery status
  • Operational capacity
  • Whether the meal has already been confirmed, prepared, reserved, or dispatched

If approved, the skipped or frozen days may be added to the end of the subscription period or handled as internal meal credit, depending on the subscription rules.

basicdite may reject freeze or skip requests submitted too late or after preparation has started.

6. Branch Pickup Policy

For branch pickup orders, a pickup request reserves selected meals from the customer's subscription balance.

If the customer does not collect the reserved meal within the available pickup window, the request may be treated as a no-show. In this case, the reserved meals may be counted as consumed and will not be eligible for cash refund.

Pickup codes are only valid for confirmed pickup requests and may be shown when the order is ready for pickup.

7. Delivery Policy

For delivery orders, the customer is responsible for providing a correct address, reachable contact information, and being available during the delivery window.

If delivery cannot be completed because of incorrect address details, unreachable contact information, customer unavailability, or refusal to receive the order, the meal may be counted as consumed and will not be eligible for cash refund.

basicdite may offer redelivery or rescheduling only where operationally possible. Additional delivery fees may apply.

8. Premium Meals & Add-ons

Premium meal upgrades and add-ons may require additional payment.

Once a premium meal or add-on is confirmed, paid, prepared, reserved for pickup, or dispatched for delivery, the related payment is non-refundable.

If payment for a premium meal or add-on is still pending, the related meal or add-on may remain unavailable until payment is completed.

9. Quality Issues or Mistakes

If there is a confirmed quality issue, missing item, incorrect meal, or operational mistake caused by basicdite, the customer should contact support as soon as possible.

basicdite will review the case and may offer, at its discretion:

  • A replacement meal
  • Internal account credit
  • Subscription extension
  • Grace day
  • Partial operational adjustment
  • Other suitable compensation

Cash refunds are not the default remedy unless basicdite determines that a refund is required for a specific exceptional case.

10. Duplicate Payments or Incorrect Charges

If a customer is charged more than once for the same transaction, or if an incorrect payment is confirmed, basicdite will review the payment records through its payment provider and internal systems.

Where a duplicate or incorrect charge is verified, basicdite may reverse, refund, or adjust the amount through the available payment method or provide an equivalent account adjustment, depending on the payment provider process and applicable requirements.

11. Payment Provider

Payments may be processed through Moyasar or other supported payment providers. basicdite does not store full payment card details on its own servers.

Refunds, reversals, or payment adjustments, where approved, may take time depending on the payment provider, bank, card issuer, or wallet provider.

12. Abuse & Fair Use

basicdite may refuse refund, replacement, freeze, skip, or compensation requests if there is suspected abuse, repeated misuse, fraudulent activity, false claims, or violation of our service terms.

13. Policy Updates

We may update this Refund, Return & Cancellation Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Continued use of basicdite after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
